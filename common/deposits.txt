Deposits
~~~~~~~~

1. First Deposit - at salvation the believer makes a deposit with the Lord. (`2 Timothy 1: 12`) You deposit your life with the Lord.

#. Second Deposit - in the Christian life the Lord deposits Bible doctrine with the believer - through teachers and personal study. (`2 Timothy 1: 14`)

#. Third Deposit - the believer in the Christian way of life deposits his fears, problems, pressures, (`1 Peter 4:19`) with the principle being found in (`1 Peter 5:7, Psalm 55:22`)

#. Fourth Deposit - the believer in the Christian way of life deposits the gospel with the unbeliever. (`Romans 1: 14`)
