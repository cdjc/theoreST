Imputation
~~~~~~~~~~

1. Abraham is the pattern of imputation of divine righteousness. (`Genesis 15:6`)

#. Divine righteousness is imputed only on the basis of faith in Christ. (`Romans 3:22`)

#. Many Gentiles of Old Testament times found God's righteousness by believing in Christ while many Jews, relying on the Law, missed imputation. (`Romans 9:30-33`)

#. Imputation is the basis of Justification. (`Romans 4:22, 5:1`)

#. Imputation encourages faith in Christ. (`Romans 4:24, 25`)

#. Imputation is based on the work of Christ on the Cross. (`2 Corinthians 5:21`)
